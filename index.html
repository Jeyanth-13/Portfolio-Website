About Jey

Vanakkam Makkaleh

As a designer,With over 4+ years of experience in UX/UI design, I approach every project with one core belief:

" Always design have purpose "

My work spans SaaS platforms, web, and mobile applications—cutting through clutter to design intuitive, human-centered interfaces. From AI-powered tools to data-driven dashboards, I translate complex systems into meaningful, effortless user experiences.

Rooted in systems thinking, I design with function in mind—letting purpose shape aesthetics. I rely on research, user flows, and clear visual hierarchy to ensure every interaction feels natural. Tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, and HTML/CSS are my daily instruments, but strategy is my foundation.

No noise. Just usable, scalable, and elegant design.

Because the best interfaces don’t just look good—they work without explanation.
